# Welcome to boobie.ai

boobie.ai is an **AI-Powered, Human-Driven™** technology company building the intelligent operating infrastructure for creators, studios, media companies, agencies, and enterprise organizations worldwide.

The **B.O.O.B.I.E.™ engine** (Back Office Operations Bot Integration Engine) is the AI architecture that powers the entire boobie.ai ecosystem — and through our API, it's available to developers building on the creative economy.

## What You Can Build

- **Workflow Automation** — Trigger intelligent workflows across 20+ creative verticals
- **Creator Management** — Provision and manage creator accounts programmatically
- **IP Registration** — Timestamp and register intellectual property with cryptographic proof
- **Webhooks & Events** — Subscribe to real-time events from the B.O.O.B.I.E.™ engine
